Portfolio Project

ABOUT

This project is a personal portfolio website developed to showcase professional profile, technical skills, and project work through a clean, structured, and visually appealing interface. The portfolio acts as a digital resume, allowing visitors to easily navigate through different sections and gain a clear understanding of the individual’s background and capabilities. The website is designed with a modern layout, smooth navigation system, and user-friendly interface to ensure an engaging browsing experience.



Functions of the Portfolio Website:

  • Home: Serves as the landing page and provides a brief introduction.
  • About: Shows background, education, skills, and interests.
  • Projects: Displays completed projects with descriptions.
  • Contact: Allows visitors to connect through contact details.
