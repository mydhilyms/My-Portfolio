Blood Donation Camp Registration System

The Blood Donation Camp Registration System is a web-based application designed to streamline and digitize the process of organizing and managing blood donation camps. The system will enable donors to register online, provide necessary personal and medical details, and select convenient donation slots. It will also help organizers efficiently manage donor records, schedule appointments, track participation, and maintain camp data in a centralized database. By replacing manual paperwork with an automated system, the project aims to improve data accuracy, save time, enhance donor management, and ensure a smooth and organized blood donation process.

System Features:

  • Donor Registration: Users can register by entering name, age, blood group, and contact details.
  • Slot Booking: Donors can choose preferred date and time for donation.
  • Data Management: Stores and manages donor records in an organized format.
  • Search & Filter: Easily find donors based on blood group or name.
  • Admin Management: Admin can view, update, and manage donor details.
  • Centralized System: All data is stored digitally for easy access and maintenance.
